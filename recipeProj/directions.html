Directions for Daylens Best Pizza

Calories 700
Total Fat 16g
Sat Fat 6g
Cholesterol 40mg
Total Carb 18g
Protein 20g
Sugar 3g

Step by Step Instuctions!

  1. Get a bowl and lightly coat it in some olive oil, Add warm water,dry yeast & sugar
  2. In another bowl combine flour and salt
  3. In the bowl containing flour and salt make a hole in the center and add the yeast, add olive oil and use a fork to pull the dry into the wet then mix.
  4. When the dough starts to come together, get in there with your hands and knead it for a few minutes on a lightly flowered board.
  5. Place the kneaded dough into the oiled bowl,cover with plastic wrap and let it rise in a warm spot untnil it doubles in size.(this should take 1-2 hours)
  6. If the dough leaves in indentation when poked, it's ready.
  7. Once the dough has risen use a knife and divide it, the largeer the peice the bigger the pizza.
  8. Shape the dough,sprinkle your pizza pan with a little semolina four a good seperation and a nutty crunch. Place the ball of dough into the center of the pan and spread using a rolling pin.(make sure the thickness is even)
  9. Top the spread dough with Pizza sauce,American Cheese and Pepperoni and a litttttle bit of granulated sugar.
  10. Bake in your oven set at 500-degrees F for approximately 10 minutes.

  11. The Best Pizza everytime! Enjoy!
